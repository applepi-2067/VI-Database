Orientation  
Summary of navX MXP board axes orientation and polarity.
Updated Dec 9, 2014 by scottlib...@gmail.com

The navX MXP can measure a total of 9 axes (3 gyroscope axes, 3 accelerometer axes and 3 magnetometer axes). In order to effectively use the values reported by the nav6, the orientation and polarity of these 3 axes must be understood.

The standard navX MXP board orientation is with the navX MXP logo on the Rear Left.

AxisAKAPoints to...Increases as...
XPitchRight side of the navX MXPthe navX MXP right side lifts
YRollFront of the navX MXPthe navX MXP front side lifts
ZYawUp from the top of the navX MXPthe navX MXP rotates to the right.
